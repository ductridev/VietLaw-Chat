# VietLaw Guide Demo Script

## 1. Purpose

This document defines the demo script for VietLaw Guide MVP.

The goal is to help the team record a clear, short, and convincing demo video for competition submission.

The demo should show:

- the problem is real;
- the product is easy to understand;
- the AI is useful but careful;
- answers are structured;
- sources are visible;
- safety guardrails exist;
- the roadmap to Vietnamese SLM and voice accessibility is credible.

---

## 2. Demo Positioning

VietLaw Guide is not an AI lawyer.

VietLaw Guide is:

A Vietnamese legal navigation assistant.

It helps users:

- understand the type of legal issue;
- identify missing information;
- prepare documents;
- view relevant sources;
- take safe first steps;
- know when to contact a lawyer or public authority.

Core message:

VietLaw Guide does not try to replace lawyers. It helps ordinary people prepare better before they contact a lawyer, public authority, or legal aid channel.

---

## 3. Main Demo Narrative

The demo should follow this narrative:

Many Vietnamese citizens and small household businesses face legal questions but do not know where to start. Legal documents are hard to read, procedures are scattered, and generic chatbots may answer too confidently without sources.

VietLaw Guide provides a safer first layer:

1. User describes the issue in everyday Vietnamese.
2. The system classifies the legal domain and risk level.
3. The system asks clarifying questions instead of guessing.
4. The system creates a practical document checklist.
5. The system suggests safe next steps.
6. The system shows sources.
7. The system warns when professional help is needed.

## 8. What to Emphasize

Emphasize:

- Vietnamese-first legal support
- ordinary citizens and small household businesses
- source-grounded answers
- safety and escalation
- practical checklist
- not replacing lawyers
- roadmap to Vietnamese SLM
- roadmap to voice accessibility

Do not over-emphasize:

- model performance
- legal correctness beyond source
- replacing lawyers
- full legal automation
- full legal database
- production readiness

---

## 9. What Not to Say

Do not say:

- AI luật sư
- Thay thế luật sư
- Tư vấn pháp lý chính thức
- Đảm bảo đúng pháp luật
- Đảm bảo thắng kiện
- Tự động xử lý tranh chấp
- Bao phủ toàn bộ luật Việt Nam
- Không cần gặp luật sư

Use instead:

- Trợ lý định hướng pháp lý ban đầu
- Không thay thế tư vấn pháp lý chính thức
- Hỗ trợ chuẩn bị giấy tờ và bước đầu
- Khuyến nghị gặp luật sư/cơ quan chức năng khi rủi ro cao
- Bản demo có phạm vi giới hạn

## 10. Screen Recording Checklist

Before recording:

- Backend is running.
- Frontend is running.
- API key is not visible.
- Terminal with secrets is hidden.
- Browser zoom is readable.
- Demo buttons are visible.
- Result panel renders correctly.
- Sources panel visible.
- Safety notice visible.
- No personal data appears.
- No raw JSON unless intentionally shown.
- No console errors visible.
- No browser tabs with private information.
- Notifications are off.

---

## 11. Demo Acceptance Checklist

Before recording final demo:

- Demo 1 runs without crash.
- Demo 2 runs without crash.
- Demo 3 runs without crash.
- Optional safety demo runs safely.
- No output says "chắc chắn thắng".
- No output says "không cần luật sư".
- No output gives evasion advice.
- No output fabricates obvious source.
- Safety notice appears every time.
- Source panel appears for main demo cases.
- UI is readable on video.
- Recording is under 2 minutes.

## 13. Final Rule

The demo should feel:

- useful;
- careful;
- grounded;
- Vietnamese-first;
- socially meaningful;
- technically credible.

The demo should not feel:

- overclaimed;
- unsafe;
- generic;
- like a thin chatbot wrapper;
- like an AI lawyer pretending to replace professionals.
